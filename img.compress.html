How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor allows you to reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean better quality but larger files)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, WebP, or keep original format)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience and SEO rankings
  • Reduced bandwidth usage: Save on hosting costs and mobile data usage
  • Email attachments: Send images more quickly with smaller file sizes
  • Social media: Some platforms compress your images anyway - better to control the quality yourself
